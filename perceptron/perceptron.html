The Perceptron Unit

Multilayered neural networks are the talk of the town. However, let's not get ahead of ourselves. Instead, let's start with a discussion of the units within the network, perceptrons. Once the perceptron is understood, a neural network can follow.

This page is not meant to be an exhaustive source of perceptron knowledge. It is meant to be breif and straight to the point, contrary to most textbooks. Additionally, this page may serve as a breif introductory comparison between two great programming languages.
